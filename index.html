Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for economic transformation in Kano State by creating a vibrant ecosystem of skilled, innovative, and self-reliant entrepreneurs who drive sustainable growth and job creation.

Our Mission

To provide world-class entrepreneurship training, mentorship, and access to resources, enabling Kano's youth and women to launch and scale successful businesses that contribute to the state's economic prosperity.

A Legacy of Empowerment

The Kano Entrepreneurship Development Institute (KEDI) is at the heart of Governor Abba Kabir Yusuf's mission to revitalize the 26 entrepreneurship centers established by the Dr. Rabiu Musa Kwankwaso administration. Since its revival in 2024, KEDIN has trained and empowered over 5,870 youth and women across various sectors including poultry, livestock, fisheries, informatics, and film production.

Our mandate is clear: to equip the next generation with practical, marketable skills that reduce unemployment and foster self-reliance. We are building a sustainable entrepreneurial ecosystem for Kano State's long-term economic growth.
